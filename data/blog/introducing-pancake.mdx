# Introducing Pancake

In an era of **multichannel online selling**, managing messages, orders, and customers across platforms like **Facebook, Instagram, Shopee, Lazada** is a real challenge.
**Pancake** was built as a complete solution — highly customizable and powered by deep AI integrations — to help businesses and individual sellers **streamline operations** and **deliver better customer experiences**.

---

## 🌟 Key Features

### 1. Unified Messaging (Pancake)
- Pull every message from Facebook, Instagram, WhatsApp, Shopee, Zalo, TikTok, and more into a single inbox.
- Reduce missed messages and respond faster with purpose-built tooling and automatic agent assignment.
- Track sales performance and individual agent productivity.

### 2. Order & Inventory Management (Pancake POS)
- Create orders right inside the chat.
- Auto-sync inventory with marketplace platforms.
- Clear reporting on revenue, top sellers, and profit.
- Connect to multiple shipping carriers (GHTK, GHN, Viettel Post, …).
- Compare shipping rates and print labels directly in the app.

### 3. Marketing, Automation & AI Agent (Botcake)
- Automate customer-care messages.
- Broadcast to large audiences at once.
- Build remarketing campaigns based on lead behavior.
- Monitor chatbot conversation performance.
- Integrate AI as a fully capable agent.

### 4. Customer Relationship Management (Pancake CRM)
- Built-in support for multiple service industries.
- Manage advertising campaigns end-to-end.
- Track marketing performance.
- Monitor your sales team's workflow.
- Detailed reporting across business activities.

### 5. Website & Landing Page Builder (Webcake)
- Build SEO-ready websites and landing pages.
- Sync data directly into Pancake POS.
- Full integration with Botcake.
